[ Gasoline price RON95 decreases slightly, oil price rises again](https://baochinhphu.vn/gia-xang-giam-nhe-gia-dau-tang-tro-lai-102240404144713781.htm)

> The managing agency continues to collect a stabilization fund for fuel oil with Mazut 180CST 3.5S at a rate of 300 dong/kg, but does not do so for other products like E5RON92 gasoline, RON95, diesel 0.05S, and kerosene. The stabilization fund is not utilized for any fuel oil products. Currently, E5RON92 gasoline is priced at 23,916 dong/liter, an increase of 291 dong/liter from the base price, while RON95-3 gasoline is priced at 24,801 dong/liter, a decrease of 15 dong/liter. Most other fuel oil products have experienced a rise in prices, including diesel 0.05S at 20,980 dong/liter (an increase of 290 dong/liter), kerosene at 21,010 dong/liter (an increase of 140 dong/liter), and Mazut 180CST 3.5S at 17,290 dong/kg (an increase of 150 dong).

![ Gasoline price RON95 decreases slightly, oil price rises again](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/zoom/600_315/334894974524682240/2024/4/4/65f222ab6974c62a9f65-17122166730411636526897-0-0-471-754-crop-17122167828972104551599.jpg)

 At this management period, the managing agency still implements the collection of a stabilization fund for fuel oil with Mazut 180CST 3.5S at a rate of 300 dong/kg, without collecting a stabilization fund for other products such as E5RON92 gasoline, RON95, diesel 0.05S, and kerosene. At the same time, it continues not to use the stabilization fund for all fuel oil products.

Specifically, E5RON92 gasoline is priced at 23,916 dong/liter, an increase of 291 dong/liter compared to the current base price; RON95-3 gasoline is priced at 24,801 dong/liter, a decrease of 15 dong/liter. However, most other fuel oil products show an increasing trend in prices. Diesel 0.05S is now priced at 20,980 dong/liter, an increase of 290 dong/liter; kerosene has a new price of 21,010 dong/liter, an increase of 140 dong/liter; Mazut 180CST 3.5S is now priced at 17,290 dong/kg, an increase of 150 dong.

Giá xăng giảm nhẹ từ chiều nay - Ảnh minh hoạ

Cụ thể, xăng E5RON92 có giá 23.916 đồng/lít, sau khi tăng 291 đồng/lít so với giá cơ sở hiện hành; xăng RON95-3 ở mức 24.801 đồng/lít, sau khi giảm 15 đồng/lít.

Hầu hết các mặt hàng dầu lại có xu hướng tăng giá trở lại. Dầu diesel 0.05S lên mức 20.980 đồng/lít, tăng thêm 290 đồng/lít; dầu hỏa có giá mới 21.010 đồng/lít, sau khi tăng 140 đồng/lít; dầu mazut 180CST 3.5S tăng thêm 150 đồng, lên mức 17.290 đồng/kg.

Ở kỳ điều hành này, cơ quan quản lý vẫn thực hiện trích lập quỹ bình ổn giá xăng dầu với dầu mazut 180CST 3.5S ở mức 300 đồng/kg, không trích lập quỹ với các mặt hàng xăng E5RON92, xăng RON95, dầu diesel 0.05S và dầu hỏa. Đồng thời tiếp tục không chi sử dụng quỹ bình ổn giá với tất cả các mặt hàng xăng dầu.
