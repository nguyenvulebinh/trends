 Thanks to a strong finish, 1. FC Cologne has celebrated a redeeming win in the Bundesliga relegation battle.
Steffen Tigges (90+1) and Luca Waldschmidt (90+2) shot the FC to a 2:1 (0:1) victory against VfL Bochum after being down. With only four points left behind, Cologne can still hope for direct salvation with the first relegation playoff spot within reach. The relegation place is also within reach for the table-17.
In the upcoming matches against relegation rivals FSV Mainz 05 and against Darmstadt 98, Cologne can follow up. VfL, who defeated Bayern Munich in February and took the lead through Felix Passlack (53), has now been waiting for six games for a win and will have to look down further.
