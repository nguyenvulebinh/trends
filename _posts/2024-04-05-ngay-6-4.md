> On April 6, Hanoi and various regions in Vietnam, including the Northwest, Northeast, Thanh Hoa - Thua Thien Hue, Da Nang to Binh Thuan, Tay Nguyen, and Southern Vietnam, will experience different weather conditions. Hanoi and the Northwest region will have many clouds, light rain, fog, and hot temperatures. The Northeast region will also see clouds and light rain, while Thanh Hoa - Thua Thien Hue will have clouds, rain, and scorching heat. Da Nang to Binh Thuan will be cloudy with heavy rain and strong winds in the evening and night, followed by hot, sunny days. Tay Nguyen will have clouds, heavy rain, and hot temperatures, while Southern Vietnam will experience clouds, showers, thunderstorms, and hot, sunny days. All regions should be aware of the risk of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind during heavy rain and strong winds.

 According to the National Hydrology Meteorology Forecast Center, on April 6, the Hanoi area will have many clouds, with light rain and fog in the evening and early morning. After that, there will be rain in some places. The detailed weather forecast for regions nationwide on April 6:

Hanoi: Many clouds, with light rain and fog in the evening and early morning; after that, rain in some places. Wind is south-southeast at level 2-3. The lowest temperature will be from 24 - 26 degrees. The highest temperature will be from 28 - 30 degrees.

Northwest region: Many clouds, with light rain and fog in the evening and early morning; after that, rain in some places, decreasing cloudiness and sunshine in the afternoon. In the Northwest area, there will be clouds in the afternoon and evening, with heavy rain and strong winds in some places, hot days with scorching heat. Wind is light. During heavy rain and strong wind, there is a risk of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind. The lowest temperature will be from 23 - 26 degrees, and in the Northwest region, it will be from 19 - 22 degrees. The highest temperature will be from 30 - 33 degrees, and in the Northwest region, it will be from 35 - 37 degrees, with some places over 37 degrees.

Northeast region: Many clouds, with light rain and fog in the evening and early morning; after that, rain in some places. Wind is northeast to southeast at level 2-3. The lowest temperature will be from 23 - 26 degrees. The highest temperature will be from 27 - 30 degrees, with some places over 30 degrees.

Thanh Hoa - Thua Thien Hue: In the north, many clouds, with rain in some places, fog and light fog in the early morning; in the afternoon, decreasing cloudiness and sunshine. In the south, there are clouds, with heavy rain and strong winds in the evening and night, hot days with scorching heat. Wind is northeast to southeast at level 2-3. During heavy rain and strong wind, there is a risk of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind. The lowest temperature will be from 23 - 26 degrees. The highest temperature, in the north, will be from 30 - 33 degrees, and in the south, it will be from 34 - 37 degrees, with some places over 37 degrees.

Da Nang to Binh Thuan: Cloudy, with heavy rain and strong winds in the evening and night; sunny days, hot in the north, with scorching heat. Wind is south-southeast at level 2 - 3. During heavy rain and strong wind, there is a risk of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind. The lowest temperature will be from 25-28 degrees. The highest temperature, in the north, will be from 35 - 37 degrees, with some places over 37 degrees, and in the south, it will be from 32 - 34 degrees.

Tay Nguyen: Cloudy, with heavy rain and strong winds in the evening and night; hot days, with scorching heat, and sunny days. Wind is light. During heavy rain and strong wind, there is a risk of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind. The lowest temperature will be from 22 - 25 degrees. The highest temperature will be from 33 degrees.  - 36 degrees, with places at 37 degrees. Southern Vietnam has clouds, with showers and thunderstorms in the evening and night; hot, sunny days, with some places extremely hot. Light wind. During thunderstorms, there is a possibility of tornadoes, lightning, hail, and strong gusts of wind. The lowest temperature ranges from 25 - 28 degrees. The highest temperature ranges from 34 - 37 degrees, with some places above 37 degrees.
